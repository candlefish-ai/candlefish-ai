Current Status

Candlefish AI, LLC remains a single-member Colorado limited liability company, with me as the sole owner. This structure is required due to Morgan Stanley compliance regulations that restrict Karen's and my ability to hold joint business interests while we remain employees.

Shadow Credits System

To ensure fairness and transparency during this interim period, we have implemented a "Shadow Credits" tracking system for Tyler, Trevor, and Kendall. This system operates as follows:

How Shadow Credits Work

  • Tracking: Each of you accrues credits based on contributions to the business (time, expertise, referrals, etc.)
  • Valuation: Credits are valued at fair market rates and documented quarterly
  • Recognition: Upon structure conversion, credits convert to equity or cash compensation
  • Transparency: Quarterly statements will show each person's accumulated credits

Trigger Conditions for Full Structure Activation

The complete family ownership structure will activate when any of the following conditions are met:

  • Karen and I both leave Morgan Stanley employment
  • Candlefish AI reaches $2 million in annual recurring revenue
  • We receive a formal acquisition offer valued at $10 million or more
  • January 1, 2027 (automatic trigger date)

CANDLEFISH AI
Family Plan
Prepared by Pat | 7 August 2025

1 · Where We Are Today

Candlefish.ai is presently a single-member Colorado LLC, 100% owned and managed by me. This minimalist shell exists solely to comply with Morgan Stanley policy while Karen and I remain on their payroll; the firm forbids either of us from sharing equity or receiving compensation from an outside venture. Every expense—hosting, legal, and design—comes out of our personal accounts; there is zero revenue and no salaries.

Shadow Credits System

Because the business cannot yet pay, we are introducing Shadow Credits:

  • A Shadow Credit is a time-stamped record of real work (code commits, research, design, operations).
  • Tyler and Trevor begin logging credits immediately; Kendall may log credits whenever her medical schedule allows.
  • Credits carry no cash value now; they will convert to future equity once we are free to share ownership.

2 · Trigger for the Full Structure

If Karen and I are ever both no longer employed by Morgan Stanley (resignation, retirement, lay-off—any scenario), then:

  1. The Colorado shell merges into a waiting Delaware parent (same name).
  2. Each business line (Trophy, Paint, Brewery, etc.) receives its own subsidiary LLC.
  3. Ownership divides into two classes of equity plus one class of voting rights.

4 · Cash-Flow Waterfall

  1. Market-median salaries (Denver figures)
    • Tyler ≈ $140k when revenue appears
    • Trevor's salary once ≥ 20 hrs/wk
    • Modest founders' pay for Karen & Pat when affordable
  2. Repay founders' capital – 20% of quarterly net cash until repaid (4% simple interest)
  3. Kids' Opportunity Fund – 10% of net proceeds from any subsidiary sale
  4. Profit distribution – 70% to Class A + Class B pro-rata, 30% retained unless board releases

Consultants: Uncle Scott McIntosh (ops) and Poppy (legal) remain cash-only and together may never receive more than 10% of any year's net profit; they hold no equity or votes.

Estate Note: If Karen & Pat both die before Candlefish generates cash, 10% of estate value (including Candlefish shares) funds a Denver Foundation endowment; the remaining 90% divides ⅓-⅓-⅓ to the children via Class B units.

5 · Why This Structure Is Fair

  • Kendall keeps equal long-term upside while focusing on medicine.
  • Tyler converts unpaid nights into Class A equity and receives a real salary as soon as revenue allows.
  • Trevor accrues credits from day one, gains a guaranteed 0.5% Class A bonus at 200 credits, and can never be mathematically outpaced (credit cap = 5 × median sibling score).
  • Karen & Pat recover seed cash only after children earn salaries; joint veto protects mission until governance matures.

6 · Credit-Score Review Panel

  • Fixed Trio: Tyler (tech lead) · Poppy (external legal) · rotating sibling seat (annual)
  • Panel may override AI scores beyond ±1 SD to keep the ledger honest.

7 · Open Items – Please Confirm

  1. Shadow Credits start immediately at age 18 (confirmed for Trevor; optional for Kendall).
  2. Panel structure above acceptable?

Send any objections or tweaks by week's end; otherwise Poppy will embed these terms in the operating agreement.
